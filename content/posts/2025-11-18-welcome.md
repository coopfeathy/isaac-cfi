# Welcome to Merlin Flight Training

We're thrilled to introduce Merlin Flight Training, your premier destination for flight instruction and unforgettable aerial tours of New York City.

## Our Mission

At Merlin Flight Training, we believe that the sky should be accessible to everyone. Whether you're dreaming of becoming a licensed pilot or simply want to experience the thrill of soaring above the Manhattan skyline, we're here to make it happen.

## What We Offer

### Flight Training
Our experienced, certified instructors provide comprehensive flight training programs tailored to your goals. From introductory discovery flights to advanced instrument rating courses, we'll guide you every step of the way.

### NYC Flight Tours
Experience New York City like never before! Our scenic flight tours offer breathtaking views of:
- The Statue of Liberty
- Manhattan skyline
- Brooklyn Bridge
- Central Park
- And many more iconic landmarks

## Safety First

Safety is our top priority. Our aircraft are meticulously maintained, and all flights follow strict safety protocols. You're in good hands with our experienced team.
