# CTA Experiment Draft

## Goal

Test homepage hero CTA wording and measure which phrase gets more visitors to start and submit the discovery flight form.

## Current Variants

- Start My Pilot Application
- Get My Free Training Plan
- Start My Free Training Profile
- Fill Out My Pilot Form
- Get Free Pilot Training Details

## Events

- `cta_viewed`: visitor saw the homepage hero CTA.
- `cta_clicked`: visitor clicked the homepage hero CTA.
- `form_started`: visitor began entering an email on the discovery flight funnel.
- `form_submit_attempted`: visitor tried to submit the email form.
- `form_submitted`: email was successfully saved and the visitor moved forward.

## Google Sheet Columns
